## Kitchen Pro — Restaurant Management System

---

## Overview

**Kitchen Pro** is a restaurant operations management system designed to improve inventory control, reduce food wastage, and ensure uninterrupted kitchen operations through real-time stock tracking and automated workflows.

The platform replaces manual inventory processes with a centralized digital system that provides complete visibility into ingredient usage, stock levels, and waste. This enables restaurant managers to make faster, data-driven decisions while reducing operational risk and cost.

---

## Business Need

Restaurants frequently experience operational inefficiencies due to manual inventory tracking and limited visibility into kitchen operations. Common challenges include:

* Unexpected stock shortages during service
* Food wastage due to poor monitoring
* Time-consuming manual stock checks
* Lack of reliable inventory data
* Inefficient purchasing decisions

These issues directly impact operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and profitability.

---

## Proposed Solution

Kitchen Pro provides a centralized system that automates key kitchen and inventory processes, including:

* Real-time ingredient inventory tracking
* Automatic stock deduction during food preparation
* Waste logging with reason tracking
* Low-stock and out-of-stock alerts
* Operational dashboards and reporting

The system ensures accurate inventory management while reducing manual workload for staff.

---

## Key Business Benefits

Implementing Kitchen Pro enables restaurants to:

* Reduce food wastage
* Prevent stock shortages
* Improve inventory accuracy
* Save staff time
* Optimize purchasing decisions
* Increase operational efficiency
* Improve profit margins

## Current System Capabilities

Kitchen Pro currently provides the following core functionality:

* Secure user authentication
* Ingredient inventory management
* Recipe and consumption tracking
* Waste management and logging
* Notification and alert system
* Dashboard and operational reporting

The system is production-ready and designed for scalability and future expansion.

---

## Enhancements in Progress

The following improvements are currently being implemented to enhance operational control and automation:

**Expiry Date and Batch Tracking**
Tracking ingredient batches with expiry dates to reduce waste and improve food safety.

**Supplier and Purchase Order Management**
Managing supplier information and automating the purchasing workflow.

**Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**
Supporting multiple user roles such as Admin, Manager, Chef, and Staff with permission-based access.

These upgrades will strengthen system reliability, security, and operational efficiency.
